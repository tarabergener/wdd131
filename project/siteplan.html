Both Ends Socks & Ties

I chose this site name because this the name the business will go by. It will be easier to navigate to the site if the name describes exactly what you can purchase there.

Site Purpose

The site will be easy to navigate for users. It will allow users to shop as well as sign up for email subscriptions. The ultimate purpose is to provide a seemless shopping experience for each user, providing them with the option to filter their results, add/remove items to their cart, and get updates and deals by email.
